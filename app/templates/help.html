Goal

Use the 16 letters in the grid provided to make long words!

Rules

In a single game you'll get 10 rounds, each with a chance to make a new word. If you can't make a word with a given set of letters, you can click "PASS" to get a whole new set of letters (but you'll get zero points for that round).

Dictionary

Most dictionaries should be fine. Most Unix-ish systems have a dictionary in /usr/share/dict/words . I'm using a lightly filtered version of the Debian American English dictionary. You can download my dictionary here.

FAQ

Why the name "I can haz wordz"?

Cats are notoriously bad at spelling: I can haz cheezburger.

Why is Qu stuck together rather than just a Q

There's no words in my dictionary that have a Q that isn't immediately followed by a U. You can enter either Q or QU in the webpage form and they'll be treated equivalently.
